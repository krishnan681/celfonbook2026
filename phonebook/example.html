About Acme Plumbing & Rooter

With over 20 years of dedicated service in the local community, Acme Plumbing & Rooter has built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and unparalleled technical expertise. Our team of state-licensed master plumbers is equipped with the latest diagnostic technology to handle everything from minor leak repairs to complex commercial sewer line installations.

We understand that plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours. That's why we offer 24/7 emergency response across the tri-county area, ensuring your home or business is protected around the clock. Our commitment to transparent pricing means you'll always receive a detailed estimate before any work begins...
